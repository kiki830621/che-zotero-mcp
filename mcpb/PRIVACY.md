# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

This MCP server:
- Runs entirely locally on your Mac
- Does not collect or transmit any personal data
- Does not connect to external servers
- Only reads your local Zotero database (read-only mode)
- Embedding models run locally on Apple Silicon via MLX

## Data Storage

- Semantic search index is stored locally at `~/.config/che-zotero-mcp/`
- No data leaves your device

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services.
